Overview
########

The scope of this chapter is in detailed explanation of the block diagram of the LimeRFE board. The above mentioned block diagram is shown in Figure 3. Please keep in mind that this block diagram depicts only RF part of the board. 

.. figure:: /images/LimeRFE_v1.0_diagrams_BD.png
  :width: 600

  Figure 3: LimeRFE v1.0 board block diagram

Basically, the board comprises receiver and transmitter blocks, where both of them have Wideband, HAM and Cellular sub-blocks. These sub-blocks are not strictly divided and separated, for example HAM and Wideband have been realised under the same hardware. Photo of the LimeRFE board, with marked most important RF blocks is shown in Figure 4 and in Figure 5. 

.. figure:: /images/LimeRFE_v1.0_Wideband_Cell_HAM_top.png
  :width: 600

  Figure 4: Receiver and transmitter sub-blocks – Top view

  :width: 600

  Figure 5: Receiver and transmitter sub-blocks – Bottom view 
  (without Heat sink and RD16HHF1 transistor)
